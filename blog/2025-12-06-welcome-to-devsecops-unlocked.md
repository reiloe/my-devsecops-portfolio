## Why DevSecOps?

Traditional approaches separate development, security, and operations into silos. DevSecOps breaks down these barriers by:

1. **Shifting Security Left**: Integrating security early in the development process
2. **Automating Everything**: Using CI/CD pipelines to ensure consistent, secure deployments
3. **Continuous Monitoring**: Tracking security and performance in real-time
4. **Collaborative Culture**: Breaking down silos between teams
