# Privacy Policy

## 1. An overview of data protection

### General information

The following information provides a simple overview of what happens to your personal data when you visit this website. Personal data is all data with which you can be personally identified.

### Data recording on this website

**Who is responsible for the data recording on this website?**
The data processing on this website is carried out by the website operator. You can find their contact details in the "Legal Information" (Impressum) of this website.

**How do we collect your data?**
On the one hand, your data is collected when you communicate it to us. This can be, for example, data that you enter in a contact form.
Other data is collected automatically or with your consent by our IT systems when you visit the website. This is primarily technical data (e.g. internet browser, operating system or time of the page call). This data is collected automatically as soon as you enter this website.
